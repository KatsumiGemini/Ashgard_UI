ABOUT US

ASHGARD was founded in 2014 by a group of I.T. and Intelligence experts. ASHGARD brings together the power of operational experience with a deep understanding of open-source intelligence. ASHGARD has manufactured and developed its own products, delivering off-the-shelf solutions as well as custom-tailored alternatives for intelligence agencies. Today, our company is both an agile innovator and a heavy hitter, successfully servicing many authorized agencies in the Philippine Government and some intelligence agencies in Southeast Asia. We are confident we have what it takes to repeat that success for you.
